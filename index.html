Welcome to Padang Golf Sukajadi

At Padang Golf Sukajadi, we offer more than just a game, we provide an extraordinary golfing experience. Designed for golfers of all skill levels, our championship-level course is set amidst breathtaking landscapes, offering the perfect balance of challenge and relaxation.

Book Now

Padang Golf Sukajadi is a 18 hole championship course originally designed and created by reputed golf course designer Thomson, Wolveridge & Perrett. Due to the latest development, the course was later altered and renovated by PT. Flora Tjipta Sarana, Jakarta. Opened in 2009, Padang Golf Sukajadi is a semi private club and member of International Association of Golf Tour Operators (IAGTO), the global trade organization for the golf tourism industry.

Padang Golf Sukajadi is one complex with Radisson Golf & Convention Center Batam, a new build hotel with 240 rooms and suites. Located strategically in the heart of Batam city, offers golfers great accessibility. It takes 15 minutes from Hang Nadim Batam international airport, 10 minutes from Batam Center international ferry terminal and 10 minutes from dow ntown of Batam.

The Par 72 Padang Golf Sukajadi measures 6,340 meters with four different tee locations (Black, Blue, White and Red tees) to suit golfers of all abilities. The grass used on the course is Salam Seashore Paspalum, will be the perfect club to host a professional golf event.

Padang Golf Sukajadi has been rated #2 in 2014 Batam course rating by Golf Digest Singapore. Padang Golf Sukajadi hosted the 1st Indonesia Golf Association Amateur Open Golf Championship in 2010, the 31st Indonesia Ladies Amateur Open Golf Championship in 2015 and 24th Indonesia Men Amateur Open Golf Championship in 2017.
