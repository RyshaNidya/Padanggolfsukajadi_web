At Padang Golf Sukajadi, we offer more than just a game, we provide an extraordinary golfing experience. Designed for golfers of all skill levels, our championship-level course is set amidst breathtaking landscapes, offering the perfect balance of challenge and relaxation.

Driving range with 24 bays in the heart of Batam City. For those looking to improve their swing or simply get a quick fix of golf, our driving range is the ideal place. It meets international standards with natural grass hitting areas and ranges designed to elevate your game to the next level.
